I major in health science in Peking University,and my dream is to make the world healthier.The three remarkable scientists set shining examples for me.I hope that I can also contribute to people's health like them.

Zhong Nanshan

He is regarded as a hero when it comes to protecting people's health.Both in the SARS pandemic and the COVID-19 pandemic,he made great efforts to control the spread of the viruses,saving innumerable lives.

Tu Youyou

She is the first scientist in Chinese mainland to win a Nobel prize.For almost fifty years she had been searching for a way to cure the malaria. Finally,Artemisinin was born.Her dedication to science deserves our respect.

Wu Mengchao

He is a doctor,but he is more than a doctor.He was seen as the father of hepatobiliary surgery in China,still working when he was 90 years old.His medical skill saved many prople who otherwise would die.
